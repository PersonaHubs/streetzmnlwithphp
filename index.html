DELIVERY OPTIONS


What are StreetzMNL’s delivery options?

During checkout, we'll provide an estimated delivery date. We'll also include that date in your order confirmation email.

STANDARD

  • Free for orders of ₱7,500 or more
  • ₱250 for orders less than ₱7,500
  • Arrives in 4–7 business days

EXPRESS

  • ₱750
  • Arrives in 3–6 business days

DELIVER TO STORE

You can have your order delivered to a nearby Nike store for pick-up. Learn more about our deliver-to-store service.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

  • Orders are processed Monday–Friday, except for national holidays. Be aware that orders may experience longer processing and delivery times during holidays.

  • Orders can't be delivered to PO boxes, re-shippers or package-forwarding services.

  • Orders can't be delivered to addresses outside the country or region where they were placed as we are only in Philippines.

  • Nike By You orders arrive in two to six weeks—standard delivery times do not apply.

  • Refunds include the total value of any returned items. Please note that delivery fees are not refundable unless you receive an incorrect item.

FAQs

Standard delivery is free for orders of ₱7,500 or more, otherwise it costs ₱250. Express delivery is available for ₱750.

Currently, StreetzMNL only ships within the country. We do not offer international shipping at this time.

Unfortunately, once your order has been processed and shipped, we are unable to change the delivery address.



RETURNS


What is StreetzMNL's returns policy?

StreetzMNL.com and StreetzMNL App purchases can be returned within 30 days (some exceptions apply). To be eligible for a return, items must be in their original condition, unworn and unwashed, with complete accessories, and the original packaging and tags must also be intact and in their original condition. This policy includes custom StreetzMNL By You sneakers.

Important: To ensure a smooth return process in case of any issues, we highly recommend recording a video of the unboxing process as you receive and open your package. This video can serve as evidence if the product is damaged, incorrect, or missing items, which will help us address your concerns more efficiently.

And remember, returns are always free for StreetzMNL Members—just make sure you sign in during checkout.

So go ahead, shop with confidence.
